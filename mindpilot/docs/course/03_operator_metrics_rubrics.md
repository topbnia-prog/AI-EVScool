# Operator Metrics Rubrics

MindPilot uses metrics, not grades. Metrics show growth of operator skill.

## Six Metrics

1. AI Understanding
2. Critical Thinking
3. Prompt Quality
4. Verification Skill
5. Error Detection Speed
6. Autonomy

## Scoring Scale

Use a 0-5 scale. Never show this as a school grade.

- 0: not observed
- 1: tries with heavy support
- 2: understands with prompts
- 3: applies independently in simple cases
- 4: applies independently in new cases
- 5: explains and transfers the skill

## Scoring Flow

- The mission defines the metric focus.
- The AI mentor proposes a score and reason.
- Backend validates that required mission steps were completed.
- Admin can review or override during MVP.
- Parent sees a short summary, not raw scoring details.
- Child sees growth language, not evaluation language.

## XP Rule

XP is energy for effort. It is not intelligence and not a grade.

XP events:
- Step completed
- Reflection answered
- Hallucination caught
- Good question asked
- Streak day completed
- Retry after mistake

Rank is based on mission completion and demonstrated skills, not raw XP farming.
