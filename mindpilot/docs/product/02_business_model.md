# Business Model

## MVP Model

MindPilot starts as a freemium SaaS.

Free access:
- Parent registration
- Operator profile creation
- MindScan diagnostic
- Basic Pilot Profile
- Mission 1
- Mission 2

Paid access:
- Missions 3-30
- Full AI mentor usage inside missions
- Full metric history
- Parent weekly summaries
- Completion certificate or operator report

## Conversion Moment

Mission 3 is the first locked mission. The user should already feel value after two missions:

- AI is not magic
- AI can hallucinate
- I can catch and verify mistakes

## Israel Payments

MVP payment provider:
- PayPal first

Possible later provider:
- Rapyd for stronger local/international payment support

Not MVP:
- Stripe direct Israel merchant account

Phase 2:
- Stripe through a suitable international legal setup, only after validation.

## Pricing Rule

Prices for Israeli parents should be shown in shekels with the final price clear, including VAT handling. Avoid surprise tax additions at checkout.

## Main MVP Metrics

- Registration to MindScan completion
- MindScan to Mission 1 completion
- Mission 1 to Mission 2 completion
- Mission 2 to paid upgrade
- Weekly active operators
- Mission completion rate
- Parent summary open rate
- Safety alert rate
- Monthly churn after payment
